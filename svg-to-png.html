Frequently Asked Questions

What is an SVG file?

SVG (Scalable Vector Graphics) is an XML-based graphic format that uses mathematical equations to draw images, allowing them to scale infinitely without losing quality. They are heavily used in web design for logos and icons.

Why convert SVG to PNG?

While SVGs are great for browsers, many older applications, presentation software, and social media platforms do not natively support uploading vector files. Converting them to a transparent PNG rasterizes the math into standard pixels so the image can be used anywhere.

Can I set custom dimensions?

Yes! One of the biggest advantages of our converter is the ability to type in exact pixel dimensions (Width × Height) before conversion. We recalculate and render the SVG math at that precise resolution to guarantee a perfectly sharp PNG.
