Frequently Asked Questions

What is HEIC format?

HEIC (High-Efficiency Image Container) is Apple's high-efficiency image format used on modern iOS devices. It saves significant storage space compared to JPGs, but it isn't universally supported natively on Windows or standard web browsers without extensions.
