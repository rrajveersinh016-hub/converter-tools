Frequently Asked Questions

What is WebP?

WebP is a modern image format developed by Google that provides superior lossless and lossy compression for images on the web. While it keeps websites fast, it is not always supported by older offline image editors or viewers.

Why convert WebP to PNG?

PNG is a universally accepted image format that flawlessly supports transparency (alpha channel). Converting WebP to PNG ensures your images can be opened in absolutely any legacy software program, presentation editor, or printing shop.
