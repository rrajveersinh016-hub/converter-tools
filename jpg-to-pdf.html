What is JPG Format?

JPG (or JPEG) is far and away the most widely used image format across the globe. Thanks to its highly efficient lossy compression algorithms, it provides an exceptional balance, making it the universal standard for digital photography, internet images, and scanned physical documents.

Why Convert JPG Images to PDF?

While JPG is perfect for single images, PDF is the definitive standard format for sharing sensitive documents professionally. Combining multiple photos into a single, cohesive PDF guarantees that formatting, order, and presentation remain completely locked and consistent across all devices.
